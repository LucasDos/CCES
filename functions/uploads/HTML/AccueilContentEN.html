Welcome

The course catalogue is intended for incoming exchange students.

Please note that this catalogue lists only the most popular courses for exchange students. It does not cover all the courses offered at the University of Tours.

Please click here to access all the courses offered at the University of Tours: https://www.univ-tours.fr/formations/offre-de-formation/

It is recommended to have a B2 level of French (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) to take courses taught in French.

Exchange students can also choose several French courses at the CUEFEE, University Centre of French as a Foreign Language (study of the language, understanding of the culture) : maximum 12 ECTS credits per semester can be obtained.

The course catalogue is designed to view courses offered by the faculties of the University of Tours, at Undergraduate and Master's level. Courses can be browsed by level, semester, field of study, language of instruction, etc. Updates are made twice a year, before the beginning of each semester.
